How It Works.


Save for your next adventure automatically.

Connect your checking account to Flyt and decide how much you would like to save towards your next travel adventure.

Flyt saves for you.

Flyt's machine learning based algorithm analyzes your checking account and develops a customized cadence that transfers money from your checking account 1- 5 times a week and puts it towards your Flyt savings goal.

Access your money at any time.

When you need your savings, text Flyt and your savings will be transferred back to your checking account the next business day. Flyt allows unlimited transfers.
